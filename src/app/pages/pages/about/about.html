About Memelka Export

Your Partner in Quality

Memelka is a dynamic Direct Export and Trading Company specializing in connecting high-quality Egyptian products with the international market.

We are strategic sourcing experts and global trade partners that export high-quality dates, agricultural products, foodstuff, and industrial materials worldwide directly from trusted local manufacturers and farmers. Our approach ensures competitive prices, consistent quality, and on-time shipment with full traceability.

Our mission is to simplify the complexities of international trade, ensuring seamless, reliable, and profitable export solutions for our worldwide clients.
